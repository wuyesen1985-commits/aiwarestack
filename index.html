Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Which is better: HeyGen or Synthesia?

If you need the absolute most realistic avatars and voice cloning for marketing, HeyGen wins. However, if you are a large corporation looking for stable, secure training videos, Synthesia is the industry standard.

Can I make AI videos for free?

Yes, platforms like InVideo and Fliki offer free tiers that allow you to generate short videos, though they usually come with a watermark. You'll need a paid plan to remove it.
